Healthy Diet Plan for Diabetes

A balanced diet is crucial for managing diabetes effectively. Follow these guidelines for better health.

Balanced Meals

Focus on whole, unprocessed foods
Include variety from all food groups
Maintain regular meal times

Recommended Foods

Non-Starchy Vegetables

  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Cauliflower
  • Green beans

Lean Proteins

  • Chicken
  • Fish
  • Beans & Lentils
  • Tofu

Foods to Limit or Avoid

Sugary drinks and sodas
Processed and fast foods
Refined grains and pastries

Important Tips

  • Consult a registered dietitian for a personalized meal plan
  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly
  • Combine healthy eating with regular exercise
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
