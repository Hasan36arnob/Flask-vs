Need Help Choosing a Specialist?

Consider these factors when selecting your diabetes or hormone specialist:

  • Specific condition you need treatment for (Type 1/2 diabetes, thyroid disorders, etc.)
  • Doctor's experience and patient reviews
  • Hospital facilities and diagnostic capabilities
  • Location and convenience for regular follow-ups
  • Insurance coverage and consultation fees
